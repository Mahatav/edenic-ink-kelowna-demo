Healing

Aftercare instructions

  • Keep brows dry for 7 to 10 days, no sweating, steam, or swimming
  • Apply the provided balm with a clean cotton swab, thin layer only
  • No makeup directly on the brow area until fully healed
  • Expect flaking around day 4 to 6, let it fall off on its own
  • Avoid direct sun and tanning beds on the area for 4 weeks
  • Book the touch-up between 6 and 8 weeks for best results
Questions

Before you book

Does it hurt?

Topical numbing keeps most of the appointment comfortable. Some sensitivity toward the end is normal.

Who shouldn't get microblading?

Pregnant or nursing clients, anyone on blood thinners, and clients with active skin conditions on the brow area should wait or check with a doctor first.

How long until it's fully healed?

Surface healing takes about 10 days. Full colour settling takes 4 to 6 weeks, which is why the touch-up is booked after that window.

Can I switch between the Kelowna and Chilliwack studios?

Yes. Both studios share the same booking system, so a touch-up can be booked at whichever location is easier that week.
