The studio

Built in Chilliwack, asked for in Kelowna

This page is the longer version of how the Kelowna studio came to exist, and what stays the same wherever you book.

Enough people asked

Edenic Ink Brows opened in Chilliwack first, and for a while, that meant Kelowna clients had a choice: make the two-and-a-half-hour drive, or go somewhere local and hope the technique matched what they'd seen on Instagram.

A lot of them made the drive anyway. Word got around, more people asked, and eventually opening a second studio stopped being a question of "if" and became a question of when the schedule could handle it.

The Kelowna studio isn't a franchise or a different artist working under the same name. It's the same standards, applied closer to home. Bookings go through the same system Chilliwack clients already use.

How it works

Three things that don't change

A real consult first

Every appointment starts with mapping your natural brow shape and skin tone before anything touches skin. Rushed consults are where bad brows come from.

Healed, not drawn-on

The strokes are placed to fade into a soft, natural result over the healing period, not a bold shape meant to look finished on day one.

Aftercare that's actually followed up

You leave with written aftercare and a touch-up window, not just a business card. See the services page for the full instructions.
